How to Play

Rescue a dog by selecting the appropriate toolbelt item and then clicking on its threat. A threat will hover near a dog for a short period before disappearing. If you do not eliminate the threat in time, the dog's health will be reduced. Once a dog's health is completely gone, it is considered 'dead'. If all dogs die, then you have lost the game!

How to Play

Each time you rescue a dog, you earn coins, but you also lose a little supply from the tool you used. If you run out of a tool supply and have enough coins, you can purchase more supply by clicking on the shopping cart next to the tool supply level.
